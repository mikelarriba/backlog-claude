## Design the target architecture based on AzureLocal

Context:
It must be checked whether and how MIDAS can be moved from the DAPc environment to the AzureLocal environment.

Outcome:
Plan of the necessary activities for the migration or creation of all MIDAS-relevant components to AzureLocal. This applies to both the self-developed parts of MIDAS and the necessary replacements for platform components previously provided by DAPc. In addition, the sizing for the new platform must be worked out and the possible operating model (including service activities) defined.

Value:
Significant savings in operating costs and more flexible resource management are expected.

 * see outcome
